Internalize - Track Your Goals and Achieve Success

Internalizing your goals means focusing on what's within your control. With Internalize, you can break down high-level aspirations into manageable activities and milestones. Activities are key to building good habits and improving through repetition, whether it's practicing an instrument or mastering a new skill. Milestones are incremental steps towards achieving success, helping you stay motivated and track your progress. The app's progress bar and time guide keep you on track without requiring a rigid schedule, so you can complete your goals on time and with ease. By using these tools to track your goals, and with enough time, you can accomplish anything!

Main Features:

  • Track goals by activities, milestones, or both
  • Choose a color and icon for your goal
  • Set a target completion date for each goal
  • Monitor your progress with the progress bar or milestone indicator
  • Keep track of time with the time guide
  • Use the quick activity screen to quickly track your activities

No subscription required. Internalize offers your first 3 goals for free, and a one-time in-app purchase unlocks unlimited goal tracking. By making this purchase, you'll help support the app's ongoing development and receive future updates with new features to help you achieve your goals even more effectively.
